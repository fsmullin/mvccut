Let's Make the World Better Together

Morgan Valley Christian Church has been serving the Morgan Valley community and beyond since 1984. Our membership consists of believers from various faith backgrounds and thus we are very much a community church that spiritually nurtures all who God sends through our doors.

We are affiliated with American Baptist Churches, USA. As people of faith, we seek renewal and revitalization. Informed by our history and tradition, motivated by contemporary needs and challenges, and strengthened by God’s leading and the example and sacrifice of our Savior, we seek to bring a transforming witness to an uncharted future.

Our Mission

God has called Morgan Valley Christian Church to minister to the Morgan Valley and beyond serving as agents of Christ’s love and justice for those in need and offering a vital family of faith.

Our Vision

To build a family of devoted followers of Christ that worships God, shares Christ's hope and love, and ministers to each other's needs.

Our Values

  • We welcome everyone.
  • We value our differences.
  • We respect everyone.
  • We are a healing community.
  • We love unconditionally.
  • We break through barriers.
  • We serve each other.

A 2020 Wedding
